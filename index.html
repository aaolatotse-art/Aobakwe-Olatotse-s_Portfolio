Aobakwe Olatotse — Data Analytics & Engineering Professional.

Aobakwe Olatotse is a passionate Data Analyst and Data Engineer with a strong focus on transforming raw data into actionable insights and building efficient, scalable data solutions. With expertise across analytics, automation, and data infrastructure, Aobakwe combines technical depth with a strategic mindset to deliver reliable, data-driven outcomes for businesses.

He is proficient in Power BI for interactive dashboard creation and business intelligence reporting, and Excel for advanced data modeling, statistical analysis, and automation using formulas, pivot tables, and macros. On the engineering side, Aobakwe leverages PostgreSQL for robust database management and complex querying, ensuring data accuracy and performance optimization.

His workflow includes containerizing applications using Docker to ensure reproducibility and scalability across different environments. For orchestrating data workflows, Aobakwe uses Apache Airflow to automate and monitor end-to-end pipelines, enabling seamless data ingestion, transformation, and loading (ETL/ELT) processes.

Aobakwe is highly skilled in data cleaning and transformation, applying systematic methods to handle missing values, remove inconsistencies, and normalize data for accurate analytics. He excels at designing end-to-end data pipelines — from extraction and validation to transformation, storage, and visualization — ensuring smooth data flow and transparency at every stage. His work consistently emphasizes scalability, performance, and clarity, turning raw datasets into valuable business assets.
